Do you provide home service across Delhi NCR?

Yes, we provide 100% doorstep repair services across Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad. Our technicians arrive within 90 minutes of your booking.

How much is the visit and diagnostic fee?

We charge a nominal ₹299 for home visit and expert diagnostics. This fee is fully adjusted into your final bill once you proceed with the repair.

What is your typical repair turnaround time?

90% of our repairs (backlight, power, signal) are fixed on the same day. Complex panel bonding or motherboard work may take 24-48 hours depending on parts availability.

Do you use genuine authorized parts?

Absolutely. We only use 100% original, brand-certified components. We offer a 6-month to 1-year warranty on all newly installed parts.
